Market of Monsters

Nita Sanchez

Nita Sanchez is a fictional character that exists in Rebecca Schaffears book trilogy the Market of Monsters.

She lives in a world where creatures we consider mystical are alive, and dangerous. From unicorns to kelpies to chupacabras, all reimagined in this thrilling book. Nita's mum sells organs on the black market, Nita dissects them.

Physical Appearance

  • Brown Curly Hair
  • Medium Height
  • Brown Eyes
