The actual-play webseries has run three core campaigns (<i>Fantasy High</i>, <i>The Unsleeping City</i>, and <i>A Crown of Candy</i>), and several more Side Quests, including <i>Tiny Heist</i>, <i>Of Mice and Murder</i>, and upcoming season <i>The Seven</i>, which follow a group of NPCs from Fantasy High.
