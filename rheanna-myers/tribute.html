Natalia (Natasha) Alianovna Romanoff Tribute Page

Natalia Alianovna Romanoff was taken from her family at a young age before going through extensive training under General Dreykov and then living as the surrogate daughter of Alexei Shostakov and Melina Vostokoff, with another, younger girl by the name of Yelena Belova. The four of them lived in United States, Ohio for three years for a mission the adults were on to retrieve important information from a S.H.I.E.L.D. organisation and burn the rest.
